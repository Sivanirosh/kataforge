title: Feedback, Drafts, and Imports
---

**Run Samples** is for fast iteration: it checks the public examples in the prompt. **Submit** records a practice result and runs the kata's full test set, including hidden cases.

The **Results** page summarizes passed and failed tests for the submitted kata. Use it to decide whether to retry immediately or move on.

Drafts are local-only. If you leave a kata, the editor can restore your last draft from this browser; resetting to starter code clears that local draft for the kata.

The Library can also import a **UserKata** JSON file. Imported katas live beside the public examples in the local practice hub, so you can rehearse your own drills without adding account or telemetry steps.

Self-checks and checkpoints are short Cursus steps. They do not replace kata tests; they ask you to name the habit you just practiced before the next kata.
